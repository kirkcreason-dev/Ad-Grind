Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 11, 2026 · Last updated: June 11, 2026
Operator: Norse Innovations LLC · Contact: support@creasocamps.com

This Privacy Policy describes how Ad Grind ("the App," "we," "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our application. By using Ad Grind, you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Account information. If you create an account, we collect your email address and a hashed password via Firebase Authentication. You may also use the app without an account in local mode, in which case no personal data is collected or stored.

App usage data. We store your Grind Points balance, lifetime points, ads watched, daily activity, streak data, items owned, and task completion records in Firebase Firestore. This data is tied to your account (or anonymous session) and used solely to provide the app experience.

Advertising data. Ad Grind uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP address, advertising ID, and interaction data to serve personalized ads. This data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy at policies.google.com/privacy. You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings.

Device information. Standard technical data such as device type, operating system version, and crash reports may be collected automatically by Firebase to help us maintain and improve the app.

Analytics. Firebase Analytics collects anonymous usage statistics (screen views, session duration, feature usage) to help us understand how the app is used. This data does not identify you personally.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, operate, and improve the App
  • To save your progress, points, and unlocked items across sessions and devices
  • To display relevant advertisements through AdMob
  • To detect and prevent fraud or abuse
  • To respond to your support requests

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not use your data for purposes beyond those described here.

3. Third-Party Services

Firebase (Google LLC). We use Firebase Authentication and Firestore for account management and data storage. Firebase is governed by Google's Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. Data may be stored on Google servers in the United States.

Google AdMob. We use AdMob to display ads. AdMob may use your advertising ID and other device data to serve personalized ads. See AdMob's privacy information.

Grind Points are in-app currency only. They have no cash value and cannot be transferred, sold, or redeemed for physical goods or money.

4. Data Retention

Account data is retained until you delete your account. You can delete your account at any time from within the App (Account tab → Delete account & data) or by submitting a request at our deletion page. Upon deletion, your Firebase Authentication record and Firestore user document are permanently removed. Anonymous usage data retained by Firebase Analytics is subject to Google's standard retention policies (up to 14 months).

5. Children's Privacy

Ad Grind is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us at support@creasocamps.com and we will delete it promptly.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at support@creasocamps.com or use the in-app account deletion feature. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including Firebase's built-in security rules, HTTPS encryption, and Firestore rules that restrict each user to their own data. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, however, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Material changes will be communicated through the app or by updating the effective date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
